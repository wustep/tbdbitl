History


  • 1878 Band Founded as "Fife and Drum Corp" sponsored by the Military Department
  • 1928 Ramp entrance introduced during pregame entrance on North side of the field
  • 1936 Script Ohio first performed
  • 1965 Hang on Sloopy first performed
  • 1968 TBDBITL acronym given to band by Woody Hayes
  • 1973 First 5 women allowed into band
  • Present Over 7000 members on TBDBITL Grand Roster and 4000 living alumni
